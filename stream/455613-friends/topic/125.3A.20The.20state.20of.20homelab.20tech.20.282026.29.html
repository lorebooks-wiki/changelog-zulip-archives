Andrew O'Brien (Feb 02 2026 at 00:15):

My understanding of why Proxmox doesn’t support OCI out of the box is that running LXCs is a lot closer to VMs than Docker/Podman. And since there are already tools for managing containers through a web UI, they figure it’s better to focus on being a good hypervisor/cluster/networking thing and let people bring their own way of doing application containers.
