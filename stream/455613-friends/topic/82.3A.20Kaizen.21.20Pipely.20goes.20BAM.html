Matt Johnson (Mar 01 2025 at 17:47):

To answer @Jerod Santo's question about writing the Pipely docs on running it locally, I did write a lot of it while I was discovering and experimenting with making it work. I typically keep my notes of commands and references in a Markdown file as I go along, and the docs are the gradual process of refining those notes into something that could be useful to follow and repeat by other people (or my future self). I find it helps me capture the details of how I got somewhere, be able to easily set it aside and let go of it for any amount of time, then pick it back up at some point int he future to resume the adventures.

Some of my early rough notes (June 2024) helped in providing references to aid in discussions with @James A Rosen and @Gerhard via chat messages before the Let's Build a CDN - Part 2 video.
