The company that makes my washing machine doesn't make that model anymore, neither does the company that makes my TV, my shoes, my jackets, my doorknobs, my water bottle, my couch, my dishes, my drill etc. In many cases those companies don't even exist after 15 years let alone make the same models.

On a similar note.

About 20 years ago I bought a jacket from a NZ company. It's been a great jacket and despite regular use for all those years it's in excellent condition. The company is still around and still sells clothing but there is no way anything they make today is going to last 20 years. They don't make them in NZ anymore, they don't have the same management, they don't have the same employees, they don't even have the same shareholders.

Shame really.
