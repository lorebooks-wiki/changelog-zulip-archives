Matthew Fish (Oct 10 2024 at 14:18):

Currently, I'm about half way through the book, and my reading last night covered the 4 Disciplines of Execution. This ties into one of the main reasons I started this book, after two years of newborns I am trying to get back into my productivity.

The 4DX Principle

  • Focus on the Wildly Important
    • currently, I want to improve my reading habit.
  • Act on the Lead Measuers
    • I have started tracking undistracted time reading, this is generally about 15-30 minutes (one Pomodoro was my previous goal) with Kimai my time tracking solution
  • Keep a Compelling Scorecard
    • This one i am having a bit of trouble with, I don't know how to make completing a chapter/section/book more compelling. I used to try to treat myself to games, but found that a bit counter productive to what my goal is.
  • Create a Cadence of Accountability
    • I am implementing a personal review, to take place every Sunday at 10:00, this also piggybacks off my GTD goals, and I am partially using this group as accontability.
